Cycling Towards a Greener Tomorrow

Raising awareness of the benefits of cycling on both personal health and society, to inspire parents to make the choice to make the bike their main means of transportation

The Facts

  • “Transport accounts for nearly 25% of global carbon emissions, with 80% of people in cities choosing high-emissions-producing motor vehicles for their private transport trips.” (bicyclenetwork.com.au)
  • “if bikes and ebikes became 22% of all transport trips in all cities worldwide, greenhouse gas emissions and energy use would be 47% reduced by 2050.” (bicyclenetwork.com.au)
  • “When compared to other forms of transport, the amount of energy required to produce a bicycle is tiny. … Variations exist based on bicycle type and other external factors, but even taking this into consideration, bikes have a substantially lower embodied energy.” (Peach, 2018)

The Backstory

I've always loved riding my bike. But over the years it's become more than just a love of cycling - it's become a way of life. Together with which has come a conviction of the benefit of cycling not only on my mental and physical health, but also of its benefits for society and the environment.

Now that I’m a parent it’s a way of life that we adopt as a family. Cycling is second nature to my kids, who for the rest of their lives will consider it the first option when it comes to local transportation.
